# hack and seek guide

## hider

### curses curses

#### orange curse

you must find an orange and attach it yourself for the rest of the game.

#### newspaper curse

you must buy a newspaper and read it on a bench for 5 minutes.

#### fries curse

you must go and buy some fries and eat them before continuing.

#### taxi

you must stop walking every time you see a traditional london black cab.

#### rhyme

you must rhyme every sentence for the rest of the game.

#### signal fault

you must not use a train for 20 minutes.

### veto

skip the next question

### duplicate

duplicate a question in your hand

### time bonus

get a time bonus of a prescribed amount of time to be added to your game time at the end of the game.

## seeker

### radars

check if the hider is in the given radius from you.

### matching

#### same tube line

is the hider on the same tube line as you?

#### same london borough

is the hider in the same london borough as you?

### picture

various picture questions for more information.
